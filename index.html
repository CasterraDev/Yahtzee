• Objective: is to get as many points by rolling the five die.

    There are 13 rounds. That start off by rolling the five die. You then can reroll certain die you don't want up to three times

    The game ends when all 13 score catergories have been used

  • The top six sections score a sum of the choosen die. If the total score of the six sections is above 63. You will get an additional 35 points.

    3 of a kind: Three of the same dice. Scores: add up all the spots on the dice

    4 of a kind: Four of the same dice. Scores: add up all the spots on the dice

    Full House: Three dice showing the same number and Two dice showing a different number. Scores 25 points

    Small Straight: Any four consecutive numbers (eg. 1-2-3-4). Scores 30 points

    Large Straight: Any five consecutive numbers (eg. 2-3-4-5-6). Scores 40 points

    Chance: Any combination of dice. Scores the sum of the dice

    YAHTZEE: Five of a kind. All 5 dice showing the same number. Scores 50 points
